Are you bored sitting at home?

Why not study the Book of Genesis!

The book of Genesis is the first book in the Bible. It is full of interesting stories rooted in historical and cultural truths. It is also a spiritual book that contains the very thoughts and heart of the God who made you and me.

Why am I here?
Who am I?
What should I do with my life?

In this foundational book, we will discover that God has already answered these questions. But he wants us to come to him and see it for ourselves.

My name is Michael Burdi. I have been teaching the Bible to college students at Cal State University, Fullerton, one-to-one, for about 3 years. I am fascinated with the Bible—it's history, it's lessons, it's characters, it's perspective. That is why I teach the Bible to those who are interested.

Due to recent developments with the Coronavirus (COVID-19), I have decided to start this online Bible study as a way of reaching out to people online during this time. This is available to anyone who is looking to study the book of Genesis verse by verse for a length of time until we complete the whole book.

I will teach in a one-to-one fashion using online technology, such as Zoom, Skype, FaceTime, etc. In addition, we can communicate prayer requests, questions, announcements, and weekly testimony through email or other preferred communication.

Topics Include...

  • God created the heavens and the earth (Genesis 1)
  • God created man in his image and likeness to rule over (Genesis 1)
  • God created a paradise for the man to enjoy his life (Genesis 2)
  • God promised the Seed of redemption (Genesis 3)
  • Despite God's love, man's sin continues to grow (Genesis 4-5)
  • God judged the world for wickedness but offered a way of salvation (Genesis 6-8)
  • God's covenant with all mankind (Genesis 9)
  • The beginning of nations (Genesis 10-11)
  • One person leaves everything for God's calling (Genesis 12)
  • Living for God comes with many challenges (Genesis 13-15)
  • God allows for repentance and renewal of his covenant (Genesis 16-17)
  • Life with God is abundant, but the rich will not prosper (Genesis 18-19)
  • God is faithful to do everything he promises (Genesis 20-21)
  • God tests those who love him and blesses the ones who obey him (Genesis 22)
  • Marriage by faith in God's goodness and his purpose (Genesis 23-24)
  • And much more...
